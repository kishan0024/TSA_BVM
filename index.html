About TSA Chapter

An Ambitious venture towards eshtablishing Gujarat's first ever space Oriented Student Chapter built upon the aspirations of people who are passionate towards exploration of astronomy, Space Technology and related sciences as well as carrying the purpose of catalyzing the fascination of students towards cosmological domains.
